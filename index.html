WP Quadratum

WP Quadrarum is a WordPress plugin to display your last Foursquare checkin as a map widget, fully authenticated via OAuth 2.0.

This plugin allows you to display your last Foursquare checkin as a map widget on the sidebar of your WordPress powered site.

Setting and options include:

  1. Associate your WordPress powered site with your Foursquare account using OAuth 2.0, which keeps your personal information safe and secure.
  2. Add your authentication credentials for the Nokia Location APIs, either within the plugin's settings and options or via the WP Nokia Auth plugin.
  3. Choose the width and height of the widget and map on the sidebar.
  4. Choose the zoom level of the map display.
  5. Choose whether to show private checkins on the map.
